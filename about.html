About Blake's Team

Meet and learn about the photographer and his crew that will capture your most important moments.

Blake Hilton

Proessional Photographer
Main fields: Portrait, Family, Wedding, Sports, Landscapes
Savannah College of Art and School graduate

When I was 12, my dad gave me my first camera. It wasn't fancy or new. In fact, it was a camera my dad had used for a few years before he stored it away. But, one man's trash is another man's treasure. So, I carried my precious camera everywhere I went and took pictures of things I found amusing. That passion never faded away and I decided to carry it on through my college years as well. I began studying photography at Savannah College of Art and Design and enjoyed every bit of it. Now, I continue to explore the world of photography with my amazing team that I am deeply grateful for having. And my goal as a photographer is simple: capturing the beauty and elegance of a moment within a single photo. I hope that every picture I take becomes part of someone's life and memory.

Lauren Brown

Assistant Photographer
Main fields: Portrait, Landscapes
NYU graduate

My interest in photography started when I took photos of my dog with my disposable camera. The moments of my dog smiling kept me happy throughout my childhood, and let me realize the beauty of the short moments we usually pass. When diving deeper into the field of photography at NYU, I had a better understanding of the art and strived to glorify those moments and share them with others, now professionally. Working with Blake has been a great experience to me so far, which there is a lot I can learn from his professional attitude toward photography and inspiring work.

Aaron Smith

Photo Editor
Main fields: Graphic Design, Visual Graphics
Rhode Island School of Design student (Graphic Design major)

I am currently a senior at Rhode Island School of Design and I am grateful that I successfully got into Mr. Hilton's intern program. My interest in photography started in Middle school when I realized I can capture the beauty of the world with one click of a camera. During my high school years, I took a photography class all four years. Then, I decided to pursue my passion for photography by studying the major at Rhode Island School of Design to bring my skills to a new level.

About Blake Hilton

Blake is a photographer that took interest in the photography field since he was young. He officially dug deeper into this field when he started majoring photography at the Savannah College of Art and Design. Blake has a wide range of photography, including: portraits, family, wedding, and sports photos. He has continued to pursue his passion until now, and is still looking into a future in which he can capture special moments of others.
