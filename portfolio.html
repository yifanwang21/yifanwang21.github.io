About Blake Hilton

Blake is a photographer that took interest in the photography field since he was young. He officially dug deeper into this field when he started majoring photography at the Savannah College of Art and Design. Blake has a wide range of photography, including: portraits, family, wedding, and sports photos. He has continued to pursue his passion until now, and is still looking into a future in which he can capture special moments of others.
